Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SecureChat AI and our secure messaging platform.

How secure is SecureChat AI?

SecureChat AI uses end-to-end encryption, meaning your messages are encrypted on your device and can only be decrypted by the intended recipient. Our AI processing is done securely using Secret AI technology, ensuring your data remains private throughout.

Is my data stored on your servers?

No, we do not store your conversations on our servers. Once your session ends, your conversation history is deleted. This ensures maximum privacy and security for all users.

What is Secret AI technology?

Secret AI is a cutting-edge technology that allows AI processing to occur while maintaining privacy and security. It enables our platform to provide intelligent responses without compromising the confidentiality of your messages.

Can I use SecureChat AI on multiple devices?

Yes, SecureChat AI is accessible from any device with a web browser. Your conversations are not tied to a specific device, allowing you to use our platform wherever you are.
